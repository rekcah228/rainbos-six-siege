Naruto

Naruto is a japanese manga written by Masashi Kishimoto. And is about a young ninja who tries to become the hokage, the leader of his village.

One piece

One piece is a japanese manga written by Eiichiro Oda. And is about a world filled with pirates, with a main character who is called Luffy which set sail with dreams of becoming the pirate king.

Bleach

Bleach is a japanese manga written by Noriaki Kubo. And is about a word filled with soul reapers, where ichigo the main character tries to protect the ones around him.
